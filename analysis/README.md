## Distribution Shape

The distribution Right-skewed, the tail skews to the right: 9.64% of trips are longer than 30 minutes, but they significantly inflate the average. 
Radise between 45 min and 180 min together near by 4%.

## Key Insight

Casual riders have the highest Coefficient of variation 107.22.
That means they are unpredictable. One ride takes 5 minutes, another 3 hours. This complicates planning. Bikes are rented longer, it's harder to predict client rotation and there's a higher risk of bike shortages during peak hours.
